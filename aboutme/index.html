About Me

A social sciences girl in a computer science world.
My journey learning to code with support from my cat and Kevin.

At university I earned degrees in economics, political science, and East Asian studies, but currently work as an admin for a team of software developers based on a previous degree in business administration.

Being around developers at work, as well as being engaged to a developer, piqued my interest in the world of development. I've been taking advantage of online resources, such as Codecademy and Udemy, and I'm now going back to school for cybersecurity and software development. This site is where I will practice what I'm learning and keep a record of what I've learned.
