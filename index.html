About Me

Rebekah is an organized, meticulous individual who thrives most in an environment that requires creative problem solving, analytical thinking, research, and continuous learning.

Always looking for a new challenge or learning opportunity, Rebekah has obtained degrees in business administration, economics, government and politics, and a certificate in East Asian studies. Currently, she is working on a degree in software development after being introduced to the industry through her job with a technical solutions development group.

In her current position, Rebekah tests web applications, Microsoft Office plugins, and other tools through a combination of automated and manual testing.

In her software development courses and free time, Rebekah enjoys working on projects utilizing HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Python, and/or Angular.
